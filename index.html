Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DentoMate

What is DentoMate? ▼

DentoMate is a comprehensive dental practice management software designed to streamline your practice operations. It includes patient management, treatment planning, lab operations, and medical history tracking in one integrated, easy-to-use platform specifically built for dental professionals.

Is there a free trial? ▼

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all DentoMate features. No credit card required. You'll have complete access to patient management, treatment planning, lab management, and all analytics features.

Is DentoMate HIPAA compliant? ▼

Yes, DentoMate is fully HIPAA compliant with enterprise-grade security, encrypted data storage, regular security audits, and automated backups. Your patient data is protected with military-grade encryption and regularly audited for compliance.

Can I import my existing patient records? ▼

Yes! Our migration team will help you import your existing patient records and data from your current system. The process is simple, secure, and we handle everything so you don't have to worry about data loss.

What devices can I use DentoMate on? ▼

DentoMate works on desktop computers, tablets, and smartphones. Our responsive design ensures you have a great experience whether you're using Windows, Mac, iOS, or Android devices.

How much does DentoMate cost? ▼

DentoMate offers flexible, transparent pricing starting at affordable rates for single-dentist practices and scaling based on your practice size and needs. We have no hidden fees and offer a 14-day free trial so you can test all features risk-free.
