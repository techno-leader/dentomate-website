Frequently Asked Questions

What information can I store in DentoMate?

DentoMate stores comprehensive patient information including: basic demographics (name, age, sex, address, contact), medical history (BP, diabetes, allergies, pregnancy status), and complete treatment records with charges.

What treatment types does DentoMate support?

We support all major treatment types including dental implants, orthodontics, scaling, restorations (GIC, composite, amalgam), extractions, RCT, crown & bridge work, and various surgical procedures.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required. Start managing your practice with DentoMate risk-free.

How secure is my patient data?

DentoMate is fully HIPAA compliant with end-to-end encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits. Your patient data is protected by enterprise-grade security.

Can I migrate my existing patient records?

Yes! Our migration team will help you import your existing patient records and data from your current system. The process is simple and secure.

What kind of support do you offer?

We provide 24/7 email support, comprehensive documentation, video tutorials, and dedicated onboarding for all plans.
