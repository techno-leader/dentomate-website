Comprehensive Patient Management System

Store and manage all patient information securely in one centralized location

Complete Patient Records at Your Fingertips

DentoMate's patient management system is designed to help you maintain comprehensive patient records efficiently. From basic demographics to complex medical histories, manage everything in one secure platform.

Key Features

  • Demographics Management: Store complete patient information including name, age, sex, address, and multiple contact numbers for easy communication.
  • Medical History Tracking: Record critical health information including blood pressure (high/low), diabetes status, pregnancy status, heart disease, liver disease, kidney disease, and medication history.
  • Medical Allergies: Document all patient allergies and medicinal allergies to prevent adverse reactions during treatment.
  • Patient Classification: Easily distinguish between new and old patients to personalize your approach and treatment planning.
  • Quick Access: Retrieve patient records instantly with our powerful search functionality.
  • Secure Storage: All patient data is encrypted and backed up automatically with HIPAA compliance.

Benefits of Our Patient Management System

With DentoMate's patient management, you'll streamline your practice operations and provide better patient care:

  • Improved Patient Safety: Instant access to medical history and allergies prevents dangerous drug interactions.
  • Time Savings: Eliminate manual paperwork and quickly access all patient information in seconds.
  • Better Communication: Store multiple contact numbers and preferences for effective patient outreach.
  • Compliance: Meet all HIPAA and privacy requirements with enterprise-grade security.
  • Scalability: Manage from 10 to 10,000 patients with the same ease.

Integration with Treatment Planning

Patient management works seamlessly with our treatment planning and lab management features. Medical history automatically informs treatment recommendations, and all information syncs across your practice.
