Complete Treatment Planning Solution

DentoMate's treatment planning module covers every procedure your practice performs, from simple scaling to complex surgical cases.

  • ✓ Dental Implants
  • ✓ Implant Dentures (Single, Both Arches)
  • ✓ Crown & FPD (Metal, DMLS, PFM, Zirconia, Acrylic)

Periodontics & Cleaning

  • ✓ Scaling (Multiple select options)
  • ✓ Pocket Cleaning
  • ✓ Deep Curettage

Restorative

  • ✓ Cervical Abrasion
  • ✓ GIC Restoration (Type A, Type C)
  • ✓ Composite & Amalgam

Endodontics

  • ✓ Root Canal Treatment (RCT)
  • ✓ Post & Core
  • ✓ Single Sitting RCT

Extraction & Surgery

  • ✓ Extractions (Grade 1, 2, 3, Surgical)
  • ✓ Jaw Fracture Management
  • ✓ Flap & Bone Grafting Surgery

Orthodontics

  • ✓ Full Orthodontic Cases
  • ✓ Aligner Tracking
  • ✓ Custom Appliance Management

Key Features

  • Comprehensive Procedure Tracking: Document every treatment in detail with specific notes and modifications
  • Automatic Cost Calculation: Charges automatically calculated and associated with each treatment for accurate billing
  • Treatment History: Complete audit trail of all treatments performed for compliance and patient records
  • Follow-up Management: Track follow-up appointments and outcomes for comprehensive care
  • Multi-procedure Cases: Handle complex cases with multiple procedures in a single treatment plan
